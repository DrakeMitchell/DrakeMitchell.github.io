Sticky Situation

Sticky Situation is one of my Personal Game Development projects.
---------------------------------------------------------------
The Game was my Honors Project for the UW-Stout Honors College
---------------------------------------------------------------
Sticky Situation is a Puzzle/Movment game inspired by Kuru Kuru Kuruin and Geometry Dash

Footage and Gameplay

Update 1 Out Now on Itch.io!


  • A solo dev project to recreate and expand upon the gameplay mechanics of the Kuru Kuru Kuruin game series for the GBA and GameCube.

  • This is a puzzle/movement game (and potential rage game) that has the player control a constantly spinning stick in order to navigate a maze.

    • Featured currently is 7 complete levels with 4 different obstacles and an included challenge mode.
