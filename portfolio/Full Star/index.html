Full Star

Full Star is a short game created during my GDD-325 course.
---------------------------------------------------------------
The idea was to create a short game as a kind of game jam over 2 weeks of course periods.

Footage and Gameplay

Full Star is a physics based top down game created in Godot with the objective to collect all of the star pieces in order to become a "Full Star" all while fighting the gravity of planets and other objects

Follow the constellation and avoid being sucked into any planets to complete your star!
