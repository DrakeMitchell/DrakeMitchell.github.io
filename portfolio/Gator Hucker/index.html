Game Desc

Gator Hucker is a game currently being developed for my Computer Science - Game Development capstone course
--------------------------------------------
Gator Hucker is being developed over the course of two semesters with a team of 15 people, 8 programmers and 7 artists.

You will play as an alien Alligator in a 4v4 team deathmatch style gameplay reminiscent of Overwatch 2 or Marvel Rivals with a more cartoony and casual focus.

The Online features are being created utilizing the Epic Online Services SDK built into Unreal Engine 5
